Based on a real-life US military program, this high-stakes series follows a Marine as she attempts to balance her personal and professional life as the tip of the CIA's spear in the war on terror.

ABOUT THE SERIES

Special Ops: Lioness is an American spy thriller television series created by Taylor Sheridan for Paramount+. The series is based on the real-life CIA Lioness Program, which recruits female operatives to befriend the wives and girlfriends of suspected terrorists.

The series stars Zoe Saldaña as Joe, the station chief of the Lioness Program, tasked with training, managing, and leading her female undercover operatives. Nicole Kidman plays Kaitlyn Meade, the CIA's Senior Supervisor who has Joe report directly to her.
